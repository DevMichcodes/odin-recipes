Jollof Rice

ingredients

        1 cup rice
        2 cups broth (chicken/beef)
        1 onion
        2 tomatoes
        1 medium red pepper
        1 tablespoon tomato paste
        1 Beef cube
        Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

1.Chop the onions into small pieces and fry till brown.
2.Add tomato paste,mix and let it cook on low heat for 2 minutes
3.Blend the tomato and pepper with a little water and add to the pot.
4.Cover and let it cook for about 5 minutes, till it thickens a little.
5.Add the beef cube, salt and pepper and allow to cook a little more.
6.Next add in the broth and bring to a simmer.
7.Add in the washed rice and let it boil on high heat till the water evaporates halfway.
8.Reduce the heat to low, stir a little and cover the pot with aluminium foil and let it cook till ready.

Vegetable rice

Ingredients

            
250 g Rice -
Salt to taste
2 tablespoons Oil -
Capsicum - tricolors
1/2 pc Coughette -
1 pc Carrots -
50 g Peas -
1/4 bunch Coriander -
1 pc Onion -

Cooking Instructions

1.Boil water add salt and the rice. Boil for some time until its well cooked.
2.In a separate pan heat the oil, add some onions, and all the other vegetables in order of the time they take to cook.
3.Stir fry for some time.
4.Add the cooked vegetables to the ready rice and stir until well mixed.
5.Serve with a protein and vegetable of choice. Enjoy.
